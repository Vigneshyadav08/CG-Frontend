Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Mix eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Add butter or oil on medium heat.
  3. Cook: Pour eggs and spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Add toppings when slightly cooked.
  5. Fold: Fold omelette and cook 1 minute.
  6. Serve: Serve hot and enjoy.
